01 — About

Researcher.
Professor. Leader.

DC

IFRN · Pau dos Ferros

Full professor at IFRN since 2012, holding a B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UFRN. Conducted doctoral research on energy efficiency at the Trustworthy Systems Laboratory, University of Bristol (UK, 2019–2020).

Over more than a decade, he has led high-impact projects at the intersection of high-performance computing, IoT, artificial intelligence, and data analysis — collaborating with industry, government, and academic institutions across four continents.

As a product manager, researcher and technical coordinator, he has delivered innovations ranging from smart city infrastructure to medical imaging reconstruction and national hybrid education platforms.
